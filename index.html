    Text to Bridge

    Bridge feature simplifies bridging coins between different blockchains. Users can DM the bot, request to bridge a specific amount from one chain to another, and provide a destination wallet address. The bot provides an address to send funds to, then automatically converts and transfers them to the specified wallet. It supports ETH to SOL, SOL to ETH, ETH to BTC, and BTC to ETH bridging, with plans to add more chains. Users can communicate with the bot in any language.
